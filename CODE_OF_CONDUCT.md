# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussion focused on the work.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about technical concerns.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Public sharing of private information.
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block users who
violate this code of conduct.
